Germano de Sousa

Germano de Sousa is a leading Portuguese healthcare company with extensive experience in laboratory medicine. The group has 1,600 employees and operates more than 550 collection centers and 22 laboratories specializing in clinical pathology, anatomical pathology, and genetics. Furthermore, it collaborates with various clinics and hospitals, including institutions in countries such as Spain, Angola, and Kenya.
