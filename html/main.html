Joaquim Campos MSc.

I am an engineer, researcher, and programmer by profession, and I dedicate my after-work hours to philosophy, meditation, music, and dance.

Currently, I am Co-Founder and CTO at Radiobooks, a start-up that converts text documents to speech with artificial intelligence. Prior to that, I was a researcher at the Biomedical Imaging Group, EPFL, Lausanne, Switzerland.

In September, I will join a 3-month-long intensive study program on buddhist philosophy and meditation at Tergar Insitute, Kathmandu, Nepal.

Interests

My main scientific expertise is in the fields of signal processing and artificial intelligence. Specifically, I worked in deep learning, in image and video compression, and in the development of mathematical tools for supervised learning and inverse problems.

Beyond engineering, I enjoy thinking about philosophical questions related to science, the mind, ethics, and society. I appreciate engaging in a healthy discussion, being exposed to different points of view, and, when possible, teaching the little I know. My interests extend to meditation, psychology, personal development. I have been fortunate enough to have traveled to more than 30 countries.
