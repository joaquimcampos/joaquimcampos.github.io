Biography

Academic

I obtained a Bachelor's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Insituto Superior Técnico (Universidade de Lisboa), Lisbon, Portugal, in 2016. I then to move to Lausanne, Switzerland, to pursue a Master's degree in Communication Systems at École Polytéchnique Fédérale de Lausanne (EPFL), specializing in Signals, Images, and Interfaces . During that programme, I worked as a Research Intern at Disney Research, Zurich for 6 months. After completing my Master's, in 2020, I worked as a Research Assistant at EPFL's Biomedical Imaging Group, under the supervision of Prof. Michael Unser until September 2021. In this period, I was also a teaching assistant in the courses MICRO-310 Signals and Systems I and MICRO-311 Signals and Systems II , taught by Prof. Michael Unser to the Life Sciences and Microenginneering sections.

Personal

I am interested in how the mind works and tackling philosophical questions. My interests extend to the fields of Philosophy and Psychology, personal development and spirituality. I like to think deeply about topics and question my assumptions and beliefs. I like to have my mind changed and engage in a healthy discussion.
