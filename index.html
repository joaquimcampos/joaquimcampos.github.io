In Brief...

I am an engineer, researcher, and programmer by profession, and I dedicate my after-work hours to philosophy, psychology, and meditation. I enjoy thinking about problems in a holistic manner, drinking from both ancient and modern wisdom, and considering the whole pipeline from philosophical enquiry to application and experience. Currently, I am taking a two-and-a-half year course in Buddhist philosophy and meditation at Tergar Insitute, Kathmandu, Nepal.
