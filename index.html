In Brief...

I am an engineer, researcher, and programmer by profession, and I dedicate my after-work hours to philosophy, meditation, music, and dance. Currently, I am Co-Founder and CTO at Radiobooks, a start-up that converts text documents to speech with artificial intelligence. Prior to that, I was a researcher at the Biomedical Imaging Group, EPFL, Lausanne, Switzerland. In September, I will join a 3-month-long intensive study program on buddhist philosophy and meditation at Tergar Insitute, Kathmandu, Nepal.
